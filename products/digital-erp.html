Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP)

We can help you accelerate your business by empowering employees to make smarter decisions, transform business processes faster, and promote rapid business growth.

We deploy a single, comprehensive solution - Automatically pull systems and processes together to manage business activities. Our adaptable business management solution software that handles all the resources required in the business. It does this by integrating all the information flowing through the various departments. ERP Business Applications put flexibility and scalability at the core of your business, enabling you to start quickly, grow at your own pace, and adapt in real time. Easily tailor and extend the application to meet your unique business or industry-specific needs.

Cloud vs On-premise

Our ERP systems can be deployed in any number of ways: in a public or private cloud, on premise, or in various hybrid scenarios that combine environments. Quality Designs gives you the flexibility to choose the deployment that best suits your needs, whether on-premise, in the cloud or both (hybrid). The benefits of each as follows.

  • Cloud ERP - The maintenance of the system is handled by the provider. There is lower upfront costs.
  • On-premise ERP - The ERP software is installed in your data center at the locations of your choice. The installation and maintenance of the hardware and software is client staff’s responsibility.
  • Hybrid ERP - Some clients want a combination of both to meet their business requirements. Some ERP applications and data will be in the cloud and some on premise.
